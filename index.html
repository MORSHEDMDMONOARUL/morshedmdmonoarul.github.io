ExamShield Case Study

A flagship prototype for applied computer vision.

ExamShield is the clearest proof of my research engineering direction: a deployable prototype that connects computer vision, IoT signals, monitoring interfaces, and security-aware design.

Inspect repository

Problem domain

Exploring practical AI-assisted approaches to exam monitoring and academic integrity, with attention to real-time feedback, alert control, and evidence trails.

Method

Combines YOLOv8 detection, OpenCV processing, ESP32 BLE and WiFi device signaling, cooldown alerting, CSV logs, and session reports.

Deployment path

Designed around local monitoring constraints, GPU acceleration options, Raspberry Pi thinking, Roboflow-assisted dataset work, and layouts from single camera to 16-camera sessions.

Research value

The project creates a strong bridge toward trustworthy monitoring, false-positive control, multi-camera coordination, privacy-aware design, and interpretable alerting.
